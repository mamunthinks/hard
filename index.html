Copa America 2021

Copa America 2021 is over, and Argentina is the queen of South America after 28 years, with Messi finally winning his first international title. It was a long journey with 4 group matches and three knock-out matches. But let’s overview how the tournament went. After rejecting the invitation from CONMEBOL, Qatar and Australia forced the competition to be formed of two groups of 5 teams each. Argentina, Chile, Paraguay, Bolivia, and Uruguay were in Group A.
